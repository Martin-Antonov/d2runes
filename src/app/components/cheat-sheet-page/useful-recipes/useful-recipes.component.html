Useful Recipes

Caster Amulet

Amulet+Jewel+ Perfect Amethyst+Ral
=
Caster Amulet

Caster Amulets are very powerful, because they can roll +2 to class specific skills, 10-20 FCR and with various other mods such as MF and resistances.

  • In this recipe amulets should be magical and you can use any jewel.
  • To get the +2 to class skills mod, the caster amulet should be level 90+, so the magical amulet should either be:
    1. gambled amulet (you character should be level 93+)
    2. amulet from Nihlathak (your character should be level 85+)
    3. amulet from Baal (your character should be level 81+)

Blood Gloves

Nef+Jewel+Perfect Ruby+
Heavy/SharkSkin/ Vampirebone Gloves
=
Blood Gloves

Blood Gloves are a very nice options early game due to their extra life, life leach and crushing blow. They can also roll with extra resistances.

Reroll Magic Items

3x Perfect Gems/Skulls+Magic Item
=
Rerolled Magic Item

Most commonly used to reroll Grand Charms to get the +1 to X skills. Use charms from Baal or Nihlathak for best chance to roll a godly charm.
