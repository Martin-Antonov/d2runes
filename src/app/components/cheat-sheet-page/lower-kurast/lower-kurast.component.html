Lower Kurast Rune Farming

Lower Kurast (LK for short) is a great place to farm high runes in Single Player. There are 3 or 6 super chests and each of them has only 65536 combinations (2^16). Some of these combinations contain high runes. You can roll a decent map and have 30-60 second runs where you check all 6 chests. On multiplayer, maps are rerolled every time, so this is not a viable strategy - you can still check the chests, but it will not be such a power method for farming high runes.

LK runs are also good for farming charms, jewels and gems as they appear very often in the chests.

This short guide includes everything you need to know about LK farming.

Runes

As mentioned above, there are 65536 combinations and some of them contain high runes. The type of high runes available in those chests depends on the player setting (e.g. type '/player 7' command in chat or go to settings). Your chance of getting runes does NOT depend on MF. From the table, we can see that is best to farm the chests on players 3-4 or 7-8.

Let us now take a look at some probabilities. Probability is defined as P, we assume 6 chests are farmed at 30 seconds per run.

For players 3-4 or 7-8:

  • P(Getting at least one Pul+ rune) = 0.00128 or expected 1 rune every 781 runs, appr. 6.5 hours.
  • P(Getting at least one Ist+ rune) = 0.00055 or expected 1 rune every 1820 runs, appr. 15 hours.
  • P(Getting at least one Sur or Ber) = 0.000183 or expected 1 rune every 5460 runs, appr. 45 hours.
  • P(Getting a specific rune) = 0.00009155 or expected 1 rune every 10923 runs, appr. 91 hours.

Finding Super Chests

In Lower Kurast you are looking for a campfire. There are two huts, one is to the left, the other is on top. The first contains 2 super chests, the second 1 super chest. This is illustrated in the image below:

A Perfect Map

Lower Kurast maps can spawn 2 campfires for a total of 6 super chests. In a perfect map, you will have the waypoint and the two campfires very close to it.

Start from the waypoint, go through the chests and come back to the waypoint. Teleport to Act 4, save and exit and repeat.
